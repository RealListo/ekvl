Changelog:
- Religion changes:
    - Added events that provide attributes, military and province bonuses to the following religions:
        - Alik'r, receives a 10% morale of armies bonus to their characters.
        - Satakal, receives a 10% morale of armies bonus to their characters.
        - Divine Metaphysics, receives one point of learning to their characters and a 20% tech growth and 0.05 tech gain bonus in the capital province.
        - Auriel, receives one point of learning to their characters and a 0.03 culture tech gain bonus in the capital province.
        - Green Pact, receives a 20% movement speed bonus to their characters.
        - Blacksap, receives a 20% movement speed bonus to their characters.
        - Almsivi, receives one point of learning to their characters and a 0.8 culture tech gain bonus in the capital province.
        - Nerevarine, receives one point of learning and 10% morale of armies to their characters and a 0.8 culture tech gain bonus in the capital province.
        - Sixth House, receives 10% morale of armies and 20% siege to their characters and a 0.8 culture tech gain and 10% disease resistance bonus in the capital province.
        - Reclamationist, receives one point of learning to their characters and a 0.8 culture tech gain bonus in the capital province.
        - Old Velothi, receives one point of learning to their characters and a 0.8 culture tech gain bonus in the capital province.
        - Clavicus Vile, receives a 0.015 global tax modifier to their characters.
        - Hermaeus Mora, receives one point of learning to their characters and a 50% tech growth bonus in the capital province.
        - Hircine, receives a 20% movement speed bonus to their characters.
        - Mehrunes Dagon, receives a 20% siege bonus to their characters.
        - Meridia, receives one point of stewardship to their characters and a 5% disease resistance bonus in the capital province.
        - Peryite, receives two points of martial to their characters and a 10% disease resistance bonus in the capital province.
        - Hist, receives a 20% movement speed bonus to their characters.
- Bloodlines changes:
    - Can’t be created infinitely anymore.
    - Every non martial bloodline have less required traits to make.
    - Increased the cost of creating a bloodline.
- Buildings change:
    - Special buildings can be used regardless of the owner's culture or religion.
- Naval change:
    - Ship speed has been increased from 15 to 30.
- Empire creation change:
    -No longer locked by the ruler's culture/religion. Also it now shows you the cost to make one.
- Economic change:
    - Halved the economic impact of events from the steward's oversee construction action on a province.
- Trade post change:
    - Trade posts provide 50% less income.
    - Trade zone value multiplier has been decreased to 0.5 --> 0.3.
    - Bonus to trade per income when connected to the capital has been decreased from 0.4 --> 0.2.
    - The percentage increase in cost due to distance to closest connected province has been decreased from 0.003 --> 0.002.
    - Maximum trade post base that can be owned has been increased from 1 --> 2.
    - Minimum trade post base that can be owned has been increased from 4 --> 3.
- Government change:
    - Feudal and Merchant governments can hold temples as it used to be.
- Music change:
    - Only the mod's music will play ingame.
- Wonders change:
    - Wonders can be used regardless of the owner's culture or religion.
    - The Palace of Kings in Windhelm now allows infinite sign changes with no cooldown.
- Ideal Masters change:
    - Lessened the piety loss from the Ideal Masters Oblivion invasion, the maximum amount that can be lost is capped at 96 piety per month.
- State attribute changes:
    - State Diplomacy now provides 0.5% of vassal minimum levy and 0.5% of construction time, per point of State Diplomacy.
    - State Martial now provides half the bonuses it used to give.
    - State Stewardship now provides 0.5% vassal tax and 0.5% of construction cost, per point of State Stewardship.
    - State Intrigue now provides 1% siege speed, 0.2% plot power increase and 0.2% of plot power defence, per point of State Intrigue.
    - State Learning now provides 0.03 monthly piety, from 0.02 monthly piety, per point of State Learning.
- Combat modifier change:
    - Commanders scaling impact from their martial skilled has been decreased from 0.05 --> 0.025.
- Councillor mission changes:
    - Organize the army now provides 0.5% of vassal minimum levy, multiplied by the martial stat of the Marshal.
    - Hunt apostates now provides 0.5% church tax, multiplied by the learning stat of the Court Chaplain.
- Atmora changes:
    - You can't buy both Wuuthrad and Ysgramor's Shield at the market anymore.
    - If you succeed in the Ysgramor Tomb Trials, it's no longer possible to fail the whole event chain due to a 50/50 chance at the last event.
    - Changed the event chain to start without needing Wuuthrad already.
- Bugfixes:
    - Fixed an issue when a player tries to join the Dark Brotherhood.
