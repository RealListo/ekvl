Changelog:
- Bloodlines changes:
    - Can’t be created infinitely anymore.
    - Every non martial bloodline have less required traits to make.
    - Increased the cost of creating a bloodline.
- Buildings change:
    - Special buildings can be used regardless of the owner's culture or religion.
- Naval change:
    - Ship speed has been increased from 15 to 30.
- Empire creation change:
    -No longer locked by the ruler's culture/religion. Also it now shows you the cost to make one.
- Economic change:
    - Halved the economic impact of survey events from the steward's oversee construction action.
- Trade post change:
    - Trade posts provide 50% less income.
- Government change:
    - Feudal and Merchant governments can hold temples as it used to be.
- Music change:
    - Only the mod's music will play ingame.
- Wonders change:
    - Wonders can be used regardless of the owner's culture or religion.
    - The Palace of Kings in Windhelm now allows infinite sign changes with no cooldown.
- Ideal Masters change:
    - Lessened the piety loss from the Ideal Masters oblivion invasion, the maximum is capped at -96 piety per month.
- State attribute changes:
    - State Diplomacy now provides 0.5% of vassal minimum levy and 0.5% of construction time, per point of State Diplomacy.
    - State Stewardship now provides 0.5% vassal tax and 0.5% of construction cost, per point of State Stewardship.
    - State Intrigue now provides 1% siege speed, 0.2% plot power increase and 0.2% of plot power defence, per point of State Intrigue.
    - State Learning now provides 0.03 monthly piety, from 0.02 monthly piety, per point of State Learning.
- Councillor mission changes:
    - Organize the army now provides 0.5% of vassal minimum levy, multiplied by the martial stat of the Marshal.
    - Hunt apostates now provides 0.5% church tax, multiplied by the learning stat of the Court Chaplain.
- Atmora changes:
    - You can't buy both Wuuthrad and Ysgramor's Shield at the market anymore.
    - Changed the event chain to start without needing Wuuthrad owned already.

- Bugfixes:
    - Fixed an issue when a player tries to join the Dark Brotherhood.
